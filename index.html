Our Story

Rooted in Red Soil

"Semman" refers to the rich red soil of our native land. We started with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between conscious farmers and mindful consumers. Every product on our shelf tells a story of tradition, hard work, and the rhythm of nature.

We don't just sell groceries; we revive lost habits. From stone-ground flours to wood-pressed oils, we bring the village kitchen back to your modern home.
